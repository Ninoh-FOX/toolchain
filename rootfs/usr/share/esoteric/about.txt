About
----

Esoteric is based on GMenuNX, which descended from GMenu2X, and was butchered into submission by podulator.
Website: https://github.com/podulator/esoteric

GMenuNX is a fork of GMenu2X, developed by pingflood to Retrogame RS-97, released under the GNU GPL license v2.
Website: https://github.com/pingflood/GMenuNX

GMenu2X is developed by Massimiliano "Ryo" Torromeo, and is released under the GPL-v2 license.
Website: http://mtorromeo.github.com/gmenu2x
E-Mail & PayPal account: massimiliano.torromeo@gmail.com
